Are water wars round the corner?

Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar.
These are Afro-Asian countries which export oil right? They also have another common trait, they all suffer from water scarcity. There are many reasons for water scarcity and in this essay we will look at all the reasons and see how water scarcity acts as a catalyst for water wars.

Water Scarcity

So, what even is water scarcity? Put in simple terms, it is when water is scarce in a particular region. The reasons are climate change, global warming, deforestation and population growth. Deforestation and population growth are quite straightforward but we will explore climate change and global warming in further depth. You might have noticed that when you think about countries facing water scarcity are Afro-Asian countries but in reality, almost every country faces this, especially India.
